Guide

How to Download TikTok Videos Without Watermark

Downloading a TikTok video without the watermark takes three quick steps. First, open the TikTok app or website and tap the Share icon on the video you want to save, then choose Copy Link. Next, come back to this page, paste the link into the input box at the top, and press Download Video.

Jamali4U then fetches the original file from TikTok's servers and gives you three options: the clean version with no watermark, an HD version when the source supports it, and the audio track as an MP3. Everything happens on this same page — there's no app to install and no account to create.

This method works the same way whether you're on an Android phone, an iPhone, or a desktop browser on Windows or Mac, since the download runs entirely through your browser.
